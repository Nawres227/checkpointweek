About Adriana Lima

Adriana Lima is a Brazilian supermodel and actress, widely recognized as one of the most successful and iconic models in the world. She was born on June 12, 1981, in Salvador, Bahia, Brazil.

Career Highlights

Adriana Lima began her modeling career at the age of 15 and quickly rose to fame, especially for her role as a Victoria's Secret Angel from 1999 to 2018. She has also worked with major brands like Maybelline, Givenchy, and Versace.
